# 🌲 Top 3 Outdoor-Friendly Restaurants in California

*Curated for outdoor enthusiasts who want scenic dining experiences*

---

## 1. 🌊 Albion River Inn Restaurant — Mendocino County

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Location** | Mendocino County (3 hours north of San Francisco) |
| **Setting** | Cliffside overlooking the Pacific Ocean |
| **Outdoor Appeal** | Glass-paneled dining room; outdoor patio with crashing waves below |
| **Nearby Activities** | Coastal hiking trails, Anderson Valley wineries, coastal redwoods |
| **Must-Try Dish** | Pan-roasted cod with truffle-scented rice |
| **Website** | https://www.albionriverinn.com/restaurant |

**Why outdoor enthusiasts love it:** Delivers an "edge-of-the-world" feel with dramatic ocean views. Perfect post-hike reward surrounded by nature.

---

## 2. 🏔️ The Boathouse on the Pier — South Lake Tahoe

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Location** | South Lake Tahoe |
| **Setting** | Located at the end of a 1,000-foot pier—over-the-water dining |
| **Outdoor Appeal** | Deck seating directly over crystal-clear Lake Tahoe; views of Mount Tallac |
| **Nearby Activities** | Kayaking, paddleboarding, hiking trails, beach access |
| **Must-Try** | American classics with umbrella-topped drinks |
| **Website** | https://www.tahoebeachretreat.com |

**Why outdoor enthusiasts love it:** Steps from kayaking, paddleboarding, and hiking. Unbeatable mountain/lake views from the pier.

---

## 3. 🌅 Malibu Farm Restaurant — Malibu Pier, SoCal

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Location** | Malibu Pier, Southern California |
| **Setting** | Historic pier with 360° ocean views |
| **Outdoor Appeal** | Watch surfers ride waves beneath your table; legendary sunrise breakfasts |
| **Nearby Activities** | Malibu beaches, PCH coastal drives, nearby hiking trails |
| **Must-Try Dish** | Cauliflower pizza, homemade cinnamon rolls |
| **Website** | https://www.malibufarm.com |

**Why outdoor enthusiasts love it:** Farm-to-table menu (changes weekly), casual sustainable dining, direct beach access.
